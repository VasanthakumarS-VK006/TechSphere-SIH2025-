Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about reporting, tracking, and resolving civic issues in Jharkhand

How Can We Help You?

Browse through the most frequently asked questions about CivicConnect

What is CivicConnect?
CivicConnect is an innovative digital platform launched by the Government of Jharkhand to enable citizens to report civic issues in their communities. It serves as a bridge between residents and local government departments, facilitating efficient reporting and resolution of problems related to infrastructure, sanitation, utilities, and more.
How do I report an issue?
Reporting an issue is simple:
  1. From the Home page, click on "Report an Issue"
  2. Select the appropriate category for your issue
  3. Provide specific details about the problem
  4. Add the exact location using our interactive map
  5. Attach photos or videos as evidence
  6. Submit your report
Your report is automatically routed to the correct department for action.
Can I track my report?
Yes, you can track your report in real-time. After submitting an issue, you'll receive a unique tracking ID. Use the "Track Status" option on our website or mobile app to see progress updates, department actions, and estimated resolution time. You'll also receive notifications at key stages of the resolution process.
What types of issues can I report?
You can report various civic issues including:
  • Road damage and potholes
  • Water supply problems
  • Waste management and garbage collection issues
  • Malfunctioning street lights
  • Drainage and sewerage problems
  • Parks and public space maintenance
  • Illegal construction and encroachments
What data is collected?
We collect essential information to effectively resolve issues:
  • Your contact information (name, email, phone - optional)
  • Location details of the reported issue
  • Photos/videos of the problem
  • Description of the issue
  • Category and priority level
All data is protected using industry-standard security practices and encryption protocols.
Who can see my report?
Your report is primarily visible to relevant government officials responsible for addressing the specific issue. Some information may be shared with other residents in your area to create awareness about ongoing civic works. However, personal identifiers are never publicly displayed without your consent. Aggregated, non-personal data may be used for statistical analysis and transparency reports.
How long does it take to resolve an issue?
Resolution time varies based on:
  • Type and complexity of the issue
  • Department workload and resources
  • Weather conditions (for outdoor repairs)
  • Availability of materials and equipment
Simple issues may be resolved within 24-48 hours, while complex infrastructure problems might take several weeks. You can check the expected timeline in your tracking dashboard.
Is there a mobile app available?
Yes, CivicConnect is available as a mobile app for both Android and iOS devices. You can download it from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. The mobile app offers all the features of the web platform with additional capabilities like offline reporting, push notifications, and camera integration for easier issue documentation.
What if my issue isn't resolved satisfactorily?
If you're not satisfied with the resolution:
  1. You can reopen the ticket with additional comments
  2. Escalate the issue to a higher authority within the department
  3. Contact our support team for assistance
  4. Use the feedback system to rate the resolution quality
Persistent issues may be flagged for special attention by supervisory staff.
