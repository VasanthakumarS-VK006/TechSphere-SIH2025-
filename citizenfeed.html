Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about providing feedback

How long does it take to get a response?

We acknowledge all feedback within 24 hours. For complex issues, it may take 5-7 business days to provide a detailed response or resolution update.

Is my personal information kept confidential?

Yes, your personal information is kept confidential and is only used to address your feedback. We do not share your details with third parties without your consent.

Can I track the status of my feedback?

Yes, once you submit feedback, you will receive a reference number that you can use to track the status of your submission through our online portal.

What types of feedback are most helpful?

Specific, constructive feedback with clear details and, when possible, supporting evidence (photos, documents) helps us address issues more effectively.
